In Salesforce, the cumulative cross-namespace limits for SOQL queries allow for a total of 1,100 queries to be issued across multiple namespaces in a single transaction. This limit is 11 times the per-namespace limit of 100 queries. The key point is that while there's no limit on the number of certified namespaces that can be invoked in a transaction, the total number of SOQL queries across all namespaces cannot exceed 1,100. Additionally, it's important to note that these cross-namespace limits apply only to namespaces in certified managed packages. Namespaces in non-certified packages do not have their own separate governor limits and instead count against the same limits used by the org's custom code.
